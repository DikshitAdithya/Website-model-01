About Ramsagar Warehouse and Logistics

INDIA’S PREMIER DEVELOPER OF INDUSTRIAL AND WAREHOUSING PARKS

We pioneered modern industrial & logistics real estate in India with new generation industrial parks catering to the logistics infrastructure needs of leading players in sectors such as automobiles, ecommerce, FMCG, 3PL and manufacturing among other sectors. With a portfolio of around 31 million square feet across 29 logistics and industrial parks including developed parks, as well as parks under various stages of development, we have the largest network of industrial & warehousing parks in India.

IndoSpace has established a long-term partnership with GLP, the leading global provider of modern logistics facilities and technology-led solutions. The partnership enables IndoSpace to leverage GLP’s fund management, development and operational expertise and resources, as well as GLP’s extensive global customer network, to further strengthen IndoSpace’s leadership position in India.

Our continuing efforts to transform industrial real estate has won recognition from the industry. IndoSpace has been ranked as the ‘Best Developer of Industrial/Warehouse Real Estate in India’ in 2017 by the reputed Euromoney magazine for three consecutive years. It has also won the “Star Warehousing Company of the Year” award for 2017 for the third time since 2015 at the 11th Express Logistics & Supply Chain (ELSC) conclave, as well as the CII Supply Chain and Logistics Excellence (SCALE) award for 2016.
