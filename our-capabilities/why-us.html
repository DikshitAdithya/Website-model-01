Why Us

THE ADVANTAGE OF LEADERSHIP

We draw on over 20 years of industrial real estate development experience from across the world. We are conscious of the challenges faced by our tenants and provide optimal solutions that result in more efficient operations.

Here are the strengths that make us an ideal partner for your business.

Established land bank

We have an existing land bank of zoned and entitled land across India, located near large industrial clusters, consumption centres and major highways. We continuously add new land parcels to our already existing land banks. These select parcels provide our customers prime logistics locations and allow for the ease of expansion into new markets across India.

Financial strength

IndoSpace Logistics Parks Limited is an international fund dedicated solely to investing in India. Our committed capital ensures our projects are not delayed due to a lack of funding. The fund is sponsored by Realterm Global and Everstone Capital.

Global design, development and management expertise

Our parks are designed for efficient and cost effective operations. All our parks are master planned to allow for the efficient movement and loading and unloading of trucks. Facility design includes ample clear heights, optimized column spacing, flat and super flat floors designed to support high cube racking, high dock door ratios, extensive concrete truck courts, truck parking , and common amenities. Building interiors are customised to meet tenant requirements and typically include dock levelers, fire detection and suppression systems, modern and efficient lighting, and office fit-outs. Our tenants include manufacturers, retailers, transportation companies, 3PLs and other enterprises with large-scale distribution or manufacturing space requirements. [+]

Nationwide development capabilities

We have the most experienced industrial and logistics real estate development team in India. With around 30 million square feet of operational and upcoming space across India, our national reach allows clients to work with a single development partner for all their supply chain facility needs.

Local expertise

With millions of square feet developed and under development, we have the knowledge and ability to work through the practical challenges of developing modern industrial and logistics facilities in India. Our local project partners have effective on-the-ground connections to ensure a smooth and transparent development process.
