About IndoSpace

INDIA’S PREMIER DEVELOPER OF INDUSTRIAL AND WAREHOUSING PARKS

We pioneered modern industrial & logistics real estate in India with new generation industrial parks catering to the logistics infrastructure needs of leading players in sectors such as automobiles, ecommerce, FMCG, 3PL and manufacturing among other sectors. With a portfolio of around 31 million square feet across 29 logistics and industrial parks including developed parks, as well as parks under various stages of development, we have the largest network of industrial & warehousing parks in India.

IndoSpace has established a long-term partnership with GLP, the leading global provider of modern logistics facilities and technology-led solutions. The partnership enables IndoSpace to leverage GLP’s fund management, development and operational expertise and resources, as well as GLP’s extensive global customer network, to further strengthen IndoSpace’s leadership position in India.

Our continuing efforts to transform industrial real estate has won recognition from the industry. IndoSpace has been ranked as the ‘Best Developer of Industrial/Warehouse Real Estate in India’ in 2017 by the reputed Euromoney magazine for three consecutive years. It has also won the “Star Warehousing Company of the Year” award for 2017 for the third time since 2015 at the 11th Express Logistics & Supply Chain (ELSC) conclave, as well as the CII Supply Chain and Logistics Excellence (SCALE) award for 2016.

IndoSpace Sponsors

The Everstone Group is a premier India and Southeast Asia business group and multi asset investment firm with assets under management of over US$ 4.5 billion in private equity and real estate. Everstone has developed or is developing close to 50 million square feet of retail, residential, commercial and industrial real estate projects across India. Its team is made up of investment and operations specialists with years of experience in multinational companies, and a deep understanding of the Indian consumer. Everstone invests for the long term, and forms enduring partnerships that help it build a strong platform for continuing success in the future. Everstone has been awarded 'Mid-Market Firm of the Year in Asia' for two consecutive years in a row (2016 and 2017), as well as 'Private Equity Firm of the Year in India' for seven consecutive years from 2011 to 2017 by Private Equity International.

To know more visit: www.everstonecapital.com

GLP is the leading global provider of modern logistics facilities and technology-led solutions, with US$64 billion in assets under management across its real estate and private equity segments. The Company’s real estate fund platform is one of the largest in the world, spanning 73 million square meters (785 million square feet). In 2019, GLP was recognised by Private Equity Real Estate (PERE) as Global Firm of the Year and Logistics Investor of the Year.

To know more visit: www.glprop.com

Realterm is a $4+ billion AUM real estate operator executing differentiated private equity strategies focused on durable insights into the supply and demand of real assets through the supply chain. Realterm currently manages over $1.9 billion of partner equity through three logistics-oriented commingled private equity fund series.



To know more visit: www.realterm.com
